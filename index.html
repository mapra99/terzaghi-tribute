Karl Von Terzaghi

The father of soil mechanics

Born in 1883 in Austria, Terzaghi made an inmense amount of findings to the bareyly born Theory of Soil Mechanics and Geotechnical Engineering. Preceeded by other great minds like Coulomb and Mohr, these are some of the most outstanding contributions made by Terzaghi:

  • Principle of Effective Stresses in Soils
  • Failure Surface Model and Bearing Capacity of a Shallow Foundation
  • Theory on One Dimensional Consolidation of Soft Soils
  • Early contributions on Subsidence of Consolidating Clays
